About Us

Welcome to Monse Resellers, where we turn your shopping struggles into smooth transactions (and maybe a few laughs along the way).

It all started when I landed in Nairobi, bright-eyed and ready to conquer the electronics business. But before I could even blink, friends, relatives, and total strangers somehow figured out that I was "the guy who knows where to get stuff." Suddenly, my phone was buzzing non-stop:

*"Hey, can you find me a good phone at a fair price?"*
*"I need a blender that won’t explode after one week, help me out?"*
*"Bro, do you know where I can get original AirPods that aren’t ‘AiroPods’?"*

I became the unofficial “Nairobi Plug” overnight, running errands, hunting for the best deals, and shipping items all over Kenya. And you know what? I got paid for it! That’s when it hit me—why not turn this into a full-time hustle?

So, I did what any smart entrepreneur would do: I visited every shop I could find, convinced store owners that I could bring them customers, and became an official reseller and affiliate marketer. Now, Monse Resellers is here to save you time, money, and unnecessary stress.

If you want quality products at the best prices—without stepping into a single crowded shop—just hit me up. I’ll handle the sourcing, negotiating, and delivery while you sit back and enjoy the convenience.

Monse Resellers: Your plug, your shopper, your stress reliever!
